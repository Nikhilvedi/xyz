## Managing Notifications

### Disabling Notifications

**In App:**
1. Go to Settings tab
2. Toggle "Enable Notifications" to OFF
3. All scheduled notifications are cancelled

**In iOS Settings:**
1. Open iOS Settings
2. Scroll to "Workout Summary"
3. Tap "Notifications"
4. Toggle off or customize

### Changing Schedule

1. Go to Settings tab
2. Ensure notifications are enabled
3. Tap "Day" to change day
4. Tap "Time" to change time
5. Previous schedule is automatically cancelled
6. New schedule is activated immediately
